In this third update of New York Web Buzz, you will find some colored skins:
- Shakespeare Blue (event-blue.css)
- Scarletish Red (event-red
- Ripe Lemon Yellow (event-yellow
- Buttercup Orange (event-orange.css)
- Emerald Green (event-green.css)
- Lynch Gray (event-main.css)

To use a different main skin: 

1. Find the following lines in the <head></head>:
<!-- Custom styles for this template -->
<link href="assets/css/event-main.css" rel="stylesheet">

2. And change the event-main.css to the one you want to try or use: event-blue.css/event-red.css ... etc

To change the skins for the google maps at the end of the documents:

1.Find the following lines in the <head></head>:
<!-- Custom styles for Google Map -->
<link rel="stylesheet" href="assets/plugins/custom-google-map/ggl-map-main.css" type="text/css">

2. And change the ggl-map-main.css to the one you want to try or use: ggl-map-blue.css/ ggl-map-red.css etc..
